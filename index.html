The Idea

Interested in product management, marketing, strategy, or business development roles at tech companies? But worried that your lack of coding skills or tech industry knowledge will hold you back? Here's the secret: you don't need to learn how to code to break into the tech industry.

Written by three former Microsoft PMs, Swipe to Unlock gives you a breakdown of the concepts you need to know to crush your interviews, like software development, big data, and internet security. Learn to land jobs in product management and marketing at companies like Google, Facebook, and Microsoft from authors who have gotten all three.
