Microsoft Marketing ACE (Accelerated connections and experiences) Program

Interested in Microsoft’s Marketing ACE Program? Swipe to Unlock was co-authored by a former ACE intern and fulltime offer recipient. The book offers valuable tech and business insights and concept overviews that will be extremely relevant for the Microsoft ACE interview for anyone who lacks a formal technical or business education.


More details about the Microsoft’s ACE Marketing Program can be found below:

About The Program

We need world-class marketing professionals to drive our consumer and enterprise growth. We are looking for agile and innovative thinkers to keep us on the forefront of technology and on top of the continually evolving world of marketing. Today's tighter integration of technology and marketing has created a huge opportunity for marketers to connect with our customers in new ways.

What makes Microsoft a great place for marketers?

We have amazing products used by billions of people and millions of businesses. We have an incredible diversity of products, from incumbent brands to challenger brands, in established markets and emerging markets. We have some of the strongest consumer and enterprise products in the world. With the countless unique opportunities at Microsoft, you can build your career on a range of paths to continually follow your passion. The opportunities at Microsoft are hard to match anywhere else.

How long is the ACE Program?

New college hires can enter the Marketing ACE rotation program. In this two-year rotation program, you will experience two unique marketing roles. Marketing ACE participants will have the advantage of moving faster, learning more, and building stronger foundations for their marketing careers by having multiple, facilitated experiences. New college hires can enter the Marketing ACE (Accelerated Connections and Experiences) rotation program. In this two-year rotation program, you will experience two unique marketing roles. Marketing ACE participants will have the advantage of moving faster, learning more, and building stronger foundations for their marketing careers by having multiple, facilitated experiences.

We will align your first role with your core strengths. For the second rotation and third placement, we will work with you and your manager to determine how best to round out your experience and take the next step in your career to suit your interests and goals.

Applications to this opportunity are considered for all available Marketing roles.

Specific marketing roles include: Product marketing manager, Marketing communications manager, Business planner, PR manager and Audience marketing manager.

Qualifications

  • A bachelor's degree in marketing, communications or other business relater field (within six months of graduation).
  • Inspired by the continually evolving craft of marketing.
  • Thrives and delivers results in a fast-paced, sometimes ambiguous environment.
  • Ability to manage complex projects with multiple stakeholders.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
